About GreenSpoon

Starting At the Farm

GreenSpoon only collaborates with farms which feed their animals natural grass, with no additives or chemicals to ensure you are provided with fresh and natural dairy and egg products. Produce and herbs picked at farms are never genetically modified, and only FDA approved fertilizers are used.

Nurtured with Care

GreenSpoon ensures animals are cage-free, healthy, and cared for, and are given large plots of land to herd, graze and thrive.
